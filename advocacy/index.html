what is advocacy?
public support for or recommendation of a particular cause or policy
what is a campaign?
a planned course of action formulated to achieve defined objectives
what is a strategy?
a plan of action designed to achieve a long-term or overall aim
what is a strategic advocacy campaign?
a campaign, well planned and organized, designed to change the , , and/or of a target audience
there are 9 stages to an advocacy campaign
  1. confirm that a campaign is possible
  2. set a clear, measurable goal that is achievable
  3. chart your course
  4. anticipate conditions
  5. know how to make headway
  6. prioritize your target audiences
  7. put a public face on your campaign
  8. operationalize your campaign
  9. stay on track
